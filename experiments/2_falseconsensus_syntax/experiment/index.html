In this study, we are interested in how you interpret language in insurance policies, as well as your beliefs about how others will interpret insurance policies. You'll read some short passages and answer a few questions about each. Thanks for participating!




LEGAL INFORMATION:
We invite you to participate in a research study on language interpretation and insurance policies. You will be reading a short passage and making judgments about the language in the passage.
There are no risks or benefits of any kind involved in this study.
